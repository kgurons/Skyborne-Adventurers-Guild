# 11 - Economy Design

## Primary Currency

Guild Coins are the main currency.

Visual identity: golden emerald.

Emeralds are not the primary progression economy.

## Main Guild Coin Sources

- Quests
- Dungeons
- Bosses
- Guild progress milestones
- Emergency quests

## Economy Targets

- Moderate earning pace
- Avoid excessive scarcity
- Avoid infinite trivial farming loops

## Anti-Exploit Principle

Villager grinding must never become the optimal strategy.

## Spending Priorities

- Guild services and HQ upgrades
- Expedition prep and logistics
- Rank-related unlock costs
- Specialized gear and utility access

## Stability Rules

- Reward active play loops over passive automation for core progression.
- Keep repair and preparation costs meaningful but non-punitive.
- Preserve value of both combat and non-combat contribution routes.
