# 13 - Guild HQ Layout and Decoration

## HQ Role

Guild Headquarters is the permanent heart of progression at spawn.

It must be spacious, highly decorated, and socially readable.

## Required Areas

- Guild Master Office
- Reception Hall
- Weapon Shop
- Armor Shop
- Blacksmith Workshop
- Enchanter Wing
- Magic Scholar Library
- Engineer Hangar Desk
- Warehouse and Logistics
- Tavern and Gathering Hall
- Cartographer Room
- Farmer Supply Corner
- Harbor Master Command Deck
- Historian Archive
- Hall of Heroes Trophy Gallery

## Layout Principles

- Central navigation spine for quick orientation.
- Nearby prep-services cluster for pre-expedition flow.
- Hall of Heroes as visual anchor for long-term legacy.
- Vertical expansion options for future rank unlock construction.

## Decoration Priority

Decoration quality is a critical design requirement, not optional polish.

Use approved decoration mod stack first:

- Supplementaries
- Amendments
- Decorative Blocks
- Chipped
- Handcrafted
- Another Furniture
- Beautify

## Additional Decoration Mod Policy

- Can be proposed only if stable and compatible.
- Must improve immersion or readability of HQ spaces.
- Must not significantly hurt server performance.
