# 12 - Hero Titles and Reputation

## Hero Titles

Hero Titles are achievement-driven progression markers with gameplay bonuses.

## Title Rules

- Bonus magnitude target: about 5 to 10 percent
- Only one Active Title can be equipped
- Title changes are allowed only in Hall of Heroes
- Permanent Titles and Active Titles are separate systems

## Reputation System

- Guild Reputation only increases
- Reputation never decreases
- Reputation is shared at Guild scale

## Reputation Effects

- NPC dialogue evolves as prestige rises
- New opportunities and quests unlock
- Guild prestige is reflected at HQ
- Wider world acknowledges the Guild's legacy

## Coupling Between Systems

- Trophy displays, rank-ups, and major quests should feed reputation.
- Reputation milestones can unlock title opportunities.
- Titles should reinforce role identity without forcing one meta build.
