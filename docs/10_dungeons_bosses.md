# 10 - Dungeons and Bosses

## Dungeon Philosophy

- Dungeons are expedition anchors for each progression tier.
- Route planning, resource management, and role synergy should matter.
- Clear dungeon outcomes must feed Guild rank and economy progression.

## Boss Philosophy

Normal enemies scale mainly via progression and dungeon difficulty.

Bosses can include:

- Unique abilities
- Distinct trait kits
- Multi-phase encounters
- Special mechanics requiring team coordination

## Trophy and Legacy Loop

- Boss trophies are displayed in the Hall of Heroes.
- Trophy display should unlock dialogue reactions and prestige flavor.
- High-tier trophies can be tied to title and reputation milestones.

## Encounter Tiers

- Tier 1: pattern-learning introductions
- Tier 2: positional mechanics and add pressure
- Tier 3: multi-phase endurance and execution checks
- Tier 4: signature legacy encounters for S and EX progression

## Fairness and Challenge Rules

- Encounters should challenge teamwork, not rely on one-shot frustration.
- Telegraph major mechanics clearly.
- Keep failure recoverable with KeepInventory-enabled expedition loops.
