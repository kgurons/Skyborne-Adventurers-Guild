# 06 - PMMO Progression Plan

## Global Setting

- XP Multiplier: 1.5x

## Primary Skills

- Combat
- Magic
- Engineering
- Exploration
- Mining
- Farming
- Building
- Gathering

## Philosophy

- PMMO should reward growth, not block fun.
- Players must still be able to participate in group content while specializing.
- Skill checks should gate optimization, not basic participation.

## Progression Model

- Early game: broad baseline gains across all skills
- Mid game: role identity emerges by preferred activities
- Late game: hybrid builds for high-rank expeditions

## Guild-Specific PMMO Integration

- Combat and Magic support boss and dungeon readiness.
- Engineering and Building feed airship and HQ upgrades.
- Exploration drives map completion and discovery quests.
- Mining, Farming, and Gathering supply economy and crafting loops.

## Anti-Frustration Rules

- Avoid hard lockouts on mandatory story milestones.
- Provide alternate progression routes for each major objective.
- Ensure each playstyle has at least one efficient Guild Coin income path.
