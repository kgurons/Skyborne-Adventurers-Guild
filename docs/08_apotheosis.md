# 08 - Apotheosis Integration

## Purpose

Use Apotheosis to enrich itemization depth, affixes, and high-end loot chase while preserving Guild-centric pacing.

## Integration Principles

- Tie stronger affix opportunities to higher-rank expedition content.
- Keep early progression readable and not over-randomized.
- Prevent economy collapse from uncontrolled overpowered item generation.

## Recommended Progression Bands

- Early ranks (D to C): mostly stable baseline gear with low complexity affixes.
- Mid ranks (B to A): moderate affix diversity and stronger socketing decisions.
- High ranks (S to EX): curated high-risk, high-reward rolls in boss-tier content.

## Safeguards

- Limit easiest repeat farms that can flood top-tier items.
- Prioritize boss and dungeon completion as best high-end acquisition route.
- Maintain value for crafted and quest gear so progression paths coexist.
