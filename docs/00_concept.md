# 00 - Project Concept

## Title

Skyborne Adventurers Guild

## Genre and Format

- Multiplayer RPG world for exactly 3 core players
- Persistent shared progression focused on one Guild
- Long-form campaign with repeatable expedition loops

## Core Loop

1. Live in the Guild
2. Accept Quests
3. Build and expand Airship capabilities
4. Explore regions and structures
5. Clear dungeons and defeat bosses
6. Return to Guild HQ
7. Upgrade Guild systems and rank
8. Launch next expedition at higher difficulty and scope

## Pillars

- Adventure: Every session should include meaningful travel, risk, and payoff.
- Discovery: The world should continuously present unknown locations, enemies, and lore.
- Progression: Guild rank, equipment, PMMO growth, and titles must feel visible and cumulative.
- Guild Life: HQ NPCs, meetings, social spaces, and upgrades are central gameplay.
- Cooperation: Content should reward team role synergy and coordination.
- Long-term Multiplayer: Systems should scale over many sessions without requiring hard resets.

## Non-Goals

- Not a generic kitchen-sink modpack
- Not a solo-first progression model
- Not villager trading economy centric
- Not punitive death loop gameplay

## Mandatory World Rules

- KeepInventory is enabled.
- Guild Reputation only increases.
- NPC Trust only increases.
- Guild Rank is shared by all players.

## Success Criteria

- Guild HQ becomes a lived-in hub with distinct NPC identities.
- Players organically rotate between questing, engineering, combat, and social planning.
- Expedition outcomes directly feed back into Guild expansion and next-rank goals.
