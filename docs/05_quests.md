# 05 - Quest System Design

## Quest Categories

- Main Story
- Side Quests
- Kill Quests
- Delivery Quests
- Investigation Quests
- Exploration Quests
- Dungeon Quests
- Boss Hunt Quests
- Airship Construction Quests
- Guild Development Quests
- NPC Personal Quests
- Emergency Quests
- Daily Quests
- Dispatch Quests
- Guild Meetings after Rank Up

## Structural Principles

- Every quest should map to at least one design pillar.
- Quest rewards should prioritize Guild Coin, progression, and narrative continuity.
- Repetition should be controlled through rotating objectives and locations.

## Main Story Arc Shape

1. Guild foundation and onboarding
2. First region stabilization
3. Airship prototype era
4. Mid-tier dungeon campaign
5. Legendary boss escalation
6. EX-rank legacy conclusion

## Emergency Quest Design

- Time-sensitive alerts with high urgency
- Moderate reward bonus over standard quests
- Trigger situational world events and NPC reactions

## Dispatch Quest Design

- Assign out-of-base operations between major expeditions
- Supports asynchronous progress feeling during planning sessions
- Best used for resource routing, scouting, and intel gathering themes

## Daily Quest Rules

- Lightweight objectives for momentum
- Should not become mandatory grind
- Reward lower but reliable Guild Coin income

## Completion UX

- Clear objective text
- Return-to-HQ report flow
- Post-completion world feedback through NPC dialogue updates
