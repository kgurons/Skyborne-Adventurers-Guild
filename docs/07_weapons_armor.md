# 07 - Weapons and Armor Progression

## Systems in Scope

- Simply Swords
- Better Combat
- RPG Series classes and armory modules
- Iron's Spellbooks combat paths
- Base vanilla and modded armor progression

## Design Goals

- Distinct archetypes for three-player team composition
- Clear midgame and lategame upgrade milestones
- Loot progression tied to dungeons and bosses, not passive farming exploits

## Role Archetype Baseline

- Vanguard: frontline durability and threat control
- Striker: mobility and burst damage
- Specialist: magic support, control, or utility

## Progression Sources

- Quest rewards
- Dungeon loot tables
- Boss drops and trophy-linked crafts
- Blacksmith and enchanter milestone services

## Balance Rules

- Keep new tier jumps meaningful but not invalidating prior builds instantly.
- Reserve elite modifiers and strongest effects for rank-gated content.
- Ensure all three players can remain viable with different gear identities.
